Hello!
I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the School of Mathematical & Computational Sciences (SMCS) in IACS, under the supervision of Dr. Debarshi Kumar Sanyal. I am currently working in the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP), specifically on topic models which are used to extract topics from document corpora in an unsupervised manner.

For more details, check my CV or drop a mail.

News and Updates

  • January 2023: Our paper on the analysis of the dropout effect in topic modeling got accepted at EACL'23.
  • December 2022: Our paper on the Wasserstein knowledge distillation framework for topic modeling got accepted at ECIR'23.
  • November 2022: Our paper on negative sampling for topic modeling got accepted at ICON'22.
  • May 2022: Our paper on analysis of the question hour session of the Indian Parliament using dynamic topic modeling got accepted at PoliticalNLP'22.
  • October 2020: Started my Ph.D. at IACS Kolkata
