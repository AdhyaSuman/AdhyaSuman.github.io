I’m Suman Adhya. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the School of Mathematical & Computational Sciences (SMCS) at IACS, Kolkata (2020–2025). I successfully defended my thesis, titled "Studies on Neural Topic Models for Document Corpora," on January 15, 2026, under the supervision of Dr. Debarshi Kumar Sanyal.

Currently, I am working as a Research Associate - I at IACS (since Sep 2025). My research focuses on probabilistic and neural topic modeling in Natural Language Processing (NLP), with an emphasis on improving representation quality, interpretability, and efficiency. My work has been published in venues such as ACL 2025, NAACL 2024, EACL 2023, ECIR 2023, and IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence.

I am looking for industry opportunities where I can apply my expertise in NLP, Generative AI, and Mechanistic Interpretability to solve real-world problems.

For more details, check my CV or drop a mail.
